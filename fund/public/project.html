About MicrobiomeDAO

The human microbiome‚Äîthe trillions of microorganisms living in and on our bodies‚Äîrepresents one of the most promising frontiers in medicine. Yet despite its enormous potential, microbiome research remains underfunded and fragmented across academic silos.

MicrobiomeDAO changes this by creating a decentralized research network where:

  • Researchers can access funding without institutional gatekeepers
  • Patients can contribute data and participate in governance
  • Discoveries are open-source and benefit the entire community
  • IP is tokenized and revenue flows back to contributors

Our initial focus areas include inflammatory bowel disease, depression/anxiety, obesity, and aging‚Äîconditions where microbiome interventions show tremendous promise but lack sufficient investment from traditional pharma.

Market Opportunity

The global microbiome therapeutics market is projected to reach $3.2 billion by 2030, growing at 24% CAGR. Yet current approaches are limited by:

  • Centralized R&D controlled by a few large pharma companies
  • Limited data sharing between research institutions
  • High costs and long timelines for clinical trials
  • Lack of patient involvement in research priorities

Our Thesis

Decentralized Discovery

Open collaboration accelerates discovery by 10x compared to siloed research. Our network enables any scientist to contribute and be rewarded.

Patient-Driven Research

Patients know their conditions best. By giving them governance power, we ensure research focuses on real-world impact, not just publications.

IP-NFT Monetization

Tokenizing research outputs creates liquid markets for scientific IP, enabling faster commercialization and fair value distribution.

Network Effects

Each new researcher, patient, and dataset makes the network more valuable, creating a flywheel that attracts top talent and funding.

  • Staking for access to premium data and analysis tools
  • Rewards for contributing data, research, or peer review
